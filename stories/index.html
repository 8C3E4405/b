No to drama, yes to stories!

We don't need drama in our lives as Derek Sivers explained.

I totally agree that we shouldn't seek it, but what is Drama?

Drama is the specific mode of narrative, typically fictional, represented in performance. The term comes from the Greek word δρᾶμα, drama, meaning action, which is derived from the verb δράω, draō, meaning to do or to act.

wikipedia

So refusing drama is refusing a part of life, because drama comes from acting.

Though, we shoudn't seek it. We can just do our stuff. Sometimes shit happens.

When it happens, we better tell it.

Because we love stories.
